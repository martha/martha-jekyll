Unprocrastinator

This past spring in my Web Apps class, my team of six created Unprocrastinator, an enriched to-do list application. Our goal was to research, design, and develop an application with all the expected functionality of existing to-do list apps -- such as assigning tasks to specific dates, checking them off, and sorting them by tags -- but with a smarter twist. Unprocrastinator asks users to estimate how long each task took them, and by tracking the history of previously completed tasks, it offers an estimate of how long future tasks will take, helping students to improve their time management.

Node.js | Bootstrap | Sass | QUnit
